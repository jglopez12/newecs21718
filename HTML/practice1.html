Dr. Martin Luther King Jr.

Martin Luther

Dr Martin Luther King Jr fought for the civil rights of African American.
He along with many others protested peacefully on the streets to prove the point that all races should be equal.
His actions also include the iconic "i have a dream speech that he delivered at the Lincoln Memorial in Washington DC.
